I am a master's student in Artificial Intelligence at Zhejiang University, advised within a research direction centered on embodied AI, LLM agents, and planning for open-ended environments. Before joining Zhejiang University, I received my B.Eng. in Computer Science and Technology from Xi'an Jiaotong University.

My current research focuses on how intelligent agents can reason, plan, and act in complex worlds. I am especially interested in embodied agents, symbolic world modeling, and the interaction between large language models and structured planning systems.
